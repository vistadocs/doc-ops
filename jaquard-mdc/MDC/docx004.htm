X11/SC1/1989-14
Exponentiation Operator, version 3

Discussed in: SC1 Language Specification
Proposer: Richard F. Walters
Superseded by: X11/1989-10, Exponentiation Operator, version 4
Superseded by: X11/1992-51, Exponentiation Operator, version 5
Superseded by: X11/TG6/1993-1, RMDS, version 5
Superseded by: X11/TG6/1993-3, RMDS, version 6
Superseded by: X11/TG6/1993-6, RMDS, version 7
Superseded by: X11/TG6/1994-1, RMDS, version 9
Superseded by: X11/1996-4, ANSI/MDC X11.1-1995

Submitted: 1989
Description:
Using arithmetic expressions in MUMPS is frustrated by the lack of a possiblility to use exponentiation, to raise a number to a power. This proposal adds an operator to the language that will offer this functionality.
Comments:
Pre-mtg mailing April 89. Document says Type B, but X11/SC1/88-36 was raised to a SC A in Nov 88. Passed to MDC TYPE A on 5.15.89. Will become X11/89-10.


Votes

  • In Washington, District of Columbia, on 10 November 1988, a motion before Subcommittee 1 to accept X11/SC1/1988-36, Exponentiation Operator, version 2 (also known as MDC-4) as a Type A document of Subcommittee 1 passed by a vote of 28 in favor, 1 opposed and 5 abstaining.
  • In Seattle, Washington, on 15 May 1989, a motion before the full MDC to accept this document as a Type A extension of the MUMPS Development Committee superseding X11/SC1/1988-36, Exponentiation Operator, version 2 and X11.1/1986-11, Exponentiation Operator, version 1 passed by a vote of 44 in favor, 4 opposed and 1 abstaining.
