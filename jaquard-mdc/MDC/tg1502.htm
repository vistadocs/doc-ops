SC 15 Taskgroup 2 - Object Oriented Language

Established: October 1991

Chair: David Marcus (February 1993 - October 1995)
Chuck Link (March 1996 - current)

Scope of work: X11/SC15/TG2/93-3, 24 October 1993

Consider and act on enhancements to MDC standards which enable and promote the use of object oriented technology.

Time-table: X11/SC15/TG2/93-3, 24 October 1993

Present one or more proposals of object oriented facilities to Subcommittee 15 as Type B on or before February 1995, with the intent of presenting these proposals for elevation to MDC Type A on or before October 1995.
