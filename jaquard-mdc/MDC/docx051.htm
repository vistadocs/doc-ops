  • In Chicago, Illinois, on 4 June 1995, a motion before Subcommittee 12 to accept X11/SC12/TG2/1995-2, charset ISO-8859-1-USA, version 2 as amended as a Type B document of Subcommittee 12 superseding X11/SC12/TG2/1994-7, charset ISO-8859-1-USA, version 1 passed by a vote of 5 in favor, none opposed and 2 abstaining.
  • In New Orleans, Louisiana, on 26 October 1995, a motion before Subcommittee 12 to accept this document as a Type A document of Subcommittee 12 superseding X11/SC12/TG2/1995-2, charset ISO-8859-1-USA, version 2 passed by a vote of 12 in favor, none opposed and 5 abstaining.
  • In Boston, Massachusetts, on 23 March 1996, a motion before Subcommittee 12 to recommend this document for consideration as MDC Type A superseding X11/SC12/TG2/1995-2, charset ISO-8859-1-USA, version 2 passed without dissent.
