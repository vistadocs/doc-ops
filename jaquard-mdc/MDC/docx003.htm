X11.1.1/1985-18
U[se] Command

Discussed in: SC1 Language Specification
Superseded by: X11/1990-7, ANSI/MDC X11.1-1990 Draft
Superseded by: X11/1996-4, ANSI/MDC X11.1-1995

Submitted: 1985
Description:
Meaning of argument value 0 (zero) for USE-command. Many implementations of MUMPS know the concept of a 'principle device' or an 'initial device', i.e. the device that is 'current device' at the moment that a MUMPS-job starts. The meaning of this proposal is to introduce the concept of 'principle device' into the ANSI-standard and to standardize the way of reverting to the 'principle device'.
Comments:
Dated 14 May 1985.
