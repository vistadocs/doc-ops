promote SC15/A

  • X11/SC15/1993-1, Portability Size Limit for Routines, version 4
    A motion to accept this document as a Type A document of Subcommittee 15 passed by a vote of 23 in favor, 1 opposed and 1 abstaining.

    A motion to accept this document as a Type A document of Subcommittee 15 passed by a vote of 24 in favor, none opposed and 1 abstaining.
  • X11/SC15/1993-12, Initializing Intrinsic Variables, version 5
    A motion to accept this document as a Type A document of Subcommittee 15 passed by a vote of 20 in favor, 4 opposed and 4 abstaining.

promote SC15/B

  • X11/SC15/1993-3, Lower Case Characters in names, version 4
    A motion to accept this document as amended as a Type B document of Subcommittee 15 passed by a vote of 22 in favor, 5 opposed and 3 abstaining.
  • X11/SC15/1993-6, Command Argument Indirection, version 2
    A motion to accept this document as a Type B document of Subcommittee 15 passed by a vote of 22 in favor, 1 opposed and 2 abstaining.
  • X11/SC15/1993-8, Parameter Passing Clarification, version 2

  • X11/SC15/TG8/1993-1, First Line Format, version 2
